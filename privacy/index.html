3. How We Use Your Data

  • Fulfilling ClaveShard orders and verifying eligibility
  • Maintaining server security and enforcing community rules
  • Communicating important Dominion updates and events

4. Data Retention

Order records are retained for 90 days. Server logs are purged after 30 days. Discord authentication tokens are session-only. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Third Parties

We use Nitrado for server hosting, Supabase for database infrastructure, and Discord OAuth for authentication. We do not sell your data to any third party.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request deletion

  • Right to object to non-essential processing
