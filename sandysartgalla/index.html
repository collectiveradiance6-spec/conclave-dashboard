🎨 About The Galla

Art of The Dominion

Sandy's Art Galla is TheConclave Dominion's community art gallery — a dedicated space celebrating the creative talent within our ARK community. From hand-drawn dino portraits to full digital landscapes of our favorite maps, every piece tells the story of life in the Dominion.

Submit your art in Discord. Get featured here. Earn ClaveShards (◈) for accepted submissions. This is your gallery.

🎨 How to Get Featured

Sandy's Art Galla accepts community art submissions every week. Get your work displayed here and earn ClaveShards as a thank-you.

  • Attach your artwork (PNG, JPG, or GIF)
  • Tell us the map or creature that inspired it
  • Get featured in the gallery & earn ◈ ClaveShards
