About

The Woman
Behind the Grill.

Meet Kathy Jordan — the heart and soul of Suzy Q's. Every plate is made with love, from scratch, the way food is supposed to be. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just real, honest cooking that feeds more than your body.

Born and raised in the tradition of Southern cooking, Kathy brings generations of flavor to every stop. Find her every Friday and Saturday at 1825 Old Fort Parkway — bringing the Murfreesboro community together one plate at a time.
