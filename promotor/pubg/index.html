📜 Clan Rules

Know the Rules

RULE 01
☠️ No Clan Team Killing

Absolutely zero tolerance for intentional clan team killing. Accidents reviewed by management.

☠️ Immediate Removal
RULE 02
🚪 No Lobby Locking

Locking lobbies is frowned upon. If you prefer to play exclusively with the same people, this clan may not be the right fit.

💀 Warnings
RULE 03
💬 Join Discord ASAP

As of Jan 1, 2026, Discord is REQUIRED for all active members. Get in the server immediately on joining.

💀 Warnings☠️ Removal
RULE 04
📅 30-Day Inactivity

30 days off the game = immediate removal. Submit a Leave of Absence to @Liaison for anything over 7 days.

☠️ Immediate Removal
RULE 05
🏆 Weekly Clan XP

Clan XP required weekly unless you have a Leave of Absence filed with @Liaison or @Clan Leader.

💀 Two Weeks: Warning☠️ Three Times: Removal
RULE 06
⚡ Challenge Contribution

Weekly challenge participation required unless a Leave of Absence is filed. Stay active in clan-challenge-reports.

💀 Two Weeks: Warning☠️ Three Times: Removal
RULE 07
🎮 100+ Games Per Season

Active = 100+ squad games per season (8 weeks) WITH your clan. DM W1NG5ofD3ATH for IRL exceptions.

☠️ Removal
RULE 08
📆 2 Events Per Month

Participate in at least 2 events monthly across: Leadership Class, Bootcamp, 1v1 Training, Mash-Up, Platoon Battle, or Clan Wars.

💀 Warnings☠️ Full Season Absence: Removal
RULE 09
👥 Play With Your Clan

Make a concentrated effort to squad up with Wrecking Crew members. If your buddies need a clan, submit them via recruit-requests.
