The Band of The Dominion

Sol Parade is TheConclave Dominion's official music partner — the soundtrack to late-night tames, boss raids, and community events across all 10 maps. Born from within the community, a Conclave brother's own band, Sol Parade's sound is a sonic journey through indie rock and alternative vibes that perfectly captures the energy of the Dominion.

Support real music from real people in our community. Every stream, share, and show ticket keeps the spirit of the realm alive — both in-game and out.
